## Troubleshooting

### Q: Seconds not updating?
**A:** Make sure you've clicked "Parse Logs" and have active data.

### Q: Test button not working?
**A:** Check browser sound permissions and volume. Click on the page first.

### Q: App feels slow?
**A:** Normal with 1-second updates. Use "Today Only" filter for better performance.

### Q: Can I disable seconds?
**A:** Not currently, but you can ignore them if you prefer.
